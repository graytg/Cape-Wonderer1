Privacy Policy

Cape Wanderer
Last updated: 2 March 2026

Cape Wanderer ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile travel guide app for the Western Cape, South Africa. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location data: With your permission, we use your device location to show nearby places and distances. Location is processed on-device and not stored on our servers.
  • Account information: If you choose to sign in (via email, Apple, or Google), we store your email address and a unique user ID to sync your saved places and purchase status across devices.
  • Purchase information: We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. We do not store payment card details. We receive only a confirmation of purchase status.
  • Push notification token: If you grant notification permission, we store your device push token to send whale alerts, weather warnings, and other relevant notifications.
  • Usage preferences: Your travel preferences (trip style, interests, group type) are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage and optionally synced to our database if you are signed in.
  • Device identifier: We generate an anonymous device ID to manage your trial period and app state. This is not linked to any personally identifiable information unless you sign in.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show you relevant places, distances, and recommendations near you
  • To generate personalised trip itineraries based on your preferences
  • To manage your free trial and paid access status
  • To send push notifications for alerts you have opted into
  • To sync your saved places and preferences if you are signed in

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following trusted service providers:

  • Supabase – database and authentication hosting
  • RevenueCat – in-app purchase management
  • Google Maps / Apple Maps – for opening directions links
  • Expo / EAS – app delivery and push notification infrastructure

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you have an account or have the app installed. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Cape Wanderer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure storage practices to protect your data. Local data is stored using encrypted MMKV storage on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
