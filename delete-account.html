Delete Your Account

Cape Wanderer
Last updated: 2 March 2026

You can request the deletion of your Cape Wanderer account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to capewandererapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion by email.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Saved places and road trips
  • Travel preferences and profile data
  • Push notification token
  • Purchase status record

What Is Retained

  • Anonymised, non-identifiable usage data may be retained for analytics purposes for up to 90 days
  • Financial transaction records required by law

Partial Data Deletion

If you'd like to delete only specific data (e.g. saved places or preferences) without deleting your full account, email us at capewandererapp@gmail.com and specify what you'd like removed.
